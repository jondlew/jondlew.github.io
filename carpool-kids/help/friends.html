Friends

The Friends tab shows you your current friends list. Tap on a friend to see a detail view including their kids.

Use the "+" button at the top right to invite a friend from your Contacts.

Swipe left on a row for a friend you no longer plan on carpooling with to "Unfriend" them. This option is not available for friends that you share an active carpool with.

The Sent Invites tab shows all of your sent invitations that have not been responded to yet. You can use the "Resend" button if you think the original invite was missed.

If you accidentally sent the invite to the wrong address or person, you can delete it by swiping to the left and tapping "Delete Invite".

The My Invites tab shows you friend invitations sent to you by other Carpool-Kids users. Use the "Accept" button to friend the person.

If you don't want to be friends with the inviter, slide left on the row and tap "Decline Invite".
