Friends
  • Friends are the people you carpool with now or or have carpooled with in the past. Anyone who has joined one of your carpools will show up here, as will any people who are members of a carpool that you joined. Deleting or leaving a carpool does not remove a Friend.

Friends

The Friends view has three tabs: "Friends", which lists all the people you have ever carpooled with, "My Invites", a list of all invites sent to you by others, and "Sent Invites", which lists all the outstanding invites you've sent.

Tap on an entry in the Friends list to view the contact information, rider seats, Kids and Locations for the friend. Tapping on the e-mail or phone number will let you contact the person.

If you no longer share any carpools with the person, the "Unfriend" button will be active so you can remove the friend from your list.

Map View and Directions

Tap on "Locations" to see any that have been added by the friend.

Tap on a location entry to get to the map view.

The map view will zoom into the selected location, which is displayed as a red map pin.

Tap on the map pin to show the address and Driving Directions button (car icon). Tapping on the directions button will open the iOS Maps app and provide driving directions from your current location.

Invites

The "My Invites" tab shows any unanswered invitations sent to you. A red numbered badge on the tab will indicate how many invites you have (if any).

The invitation displays the name of the person who invited you and the name of their carpool. To accept, tap the green checkmark button. To delete, tap the red trash button.

The "Sent Invites" tab shows any unanswered invitations sent by you.

The invitation displays the name of the person you invited and the name of your carpool. To resend, tap the green arrow button. To delete, tap the red trash button.

To invite a new friend, use the "Invite" button on the upper right. Like inviting from a carpool, your contact will need to have a valid e-mail address in their iOS Contact entry. If you've subscribed to a Pro account and own more than one carpool, you'll need to choose which carpool to use on the invitation.
