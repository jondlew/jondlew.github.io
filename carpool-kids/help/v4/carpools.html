Carpools
  • A carpool is made up of your friends and their kids, and serves as a template for creating Events. A carpool provides default values for "who, what and where", while the events you create for the carpool define the "when" and allow let you change the defaults as needed.

Create a Carpool

From the Main Menu go to Carpools and tap the "+" button on the upper right or the blue "Create a New Carpool" button.

Provide a name, event, location and address for the New Carpool. These values are defaults and can be changed in each event.

Keep the values short and to the point. Enable "Suppress Notifications" to avoid annoying other drivers while creating initial events.

Add a Friend

To add a Friend to your carpool, you need to invite them. Tap the blue "+" button in the "Add a Friend" section of your saved carpool.

Tap the Invite button on the upper right or use the blue "Invite a Friend" button.

From your Contacts select a friend to invite. Contacts must have an e-mail address in their iOS Contact entry to be selected. Contacts missing an e-mail address will be grayed out.

Selecting a valid contact will display the Send Invitation dialog, where you can choose to Send Now or cancel the sending of the invitation. The invitation will be delivered by e-mail to the contact.

Once your friend creates a Carpool-Kids account and accepts your invite, they will be automatically added to your carpool and show up in your Friends list.

Add a Kid

In the "Add a Kid" section of the carpool, tap the blue "+" button. As the carpool Owner, you have the ability to assign both your kids and any kids from friends who have joined the carpool.

Tap on a kid to add. You will have a choice to add the kid to just the carpool, or to the carpool AND all future events. At this point you haven't created any events, so selecting "Carpool Only" is fine. In the future, if a new kid is added, you will probably want to choose "Carpool and Future Events"

Once you've added kids, your carpool should look something like this. As additional friends accept your invites, you'll return here to add their kids, or you can let them add their kids themselves.

Editing a Carpool

As the carpool Owner, if you need to make any changes to the carpool name or default event, location or address, tap the pencil button on the upper right.

To remove a driver or rider, tap on their avatar to select, then use the red "-" button in the corresponding section to delete.

If you've joined a carpool and want to leave it, use the delete button on the upper right. As a joiner, you aren't able to invite or edit the Friends section, but you can add or remove your own kids.
